- "Cool Comfort" → Your Company Name
- "(555) 123-4567" → Your Phone Number
- "info@example.com" → Your Email
- "Greater Metropolitan Area" → Your Service Area

### 2. Brand Colors
In the `<style>` section, find `:root { }`:
```css

    --accent: #FF6B35;         /* Change this */
    --dark: #1A1A1A;
```

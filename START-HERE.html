❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use these commercially? Yes! All templates are free to use for personal and commercial projects.
Do I need to change the design? Recommended but optional. Update colors, fonts, and images to match your brand.
Are these SEO-optimized? Yes, but update titles, descriptions, and content for your specific keywords.
Can I add more sections? Absolutely! Copy any section you like and customize it to your needs.
How do I make contact forms work? Use Formspree (easiest), EmailJS, or your own backend server.
Can I combine multiple templates? Yes! You can mix and match sections from different templates.
What hosting should I use? Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages (free), or traditional hosting ($2-5/month).
How do I deploy these? Simply upload the HTML file to your hosting or use drag-and-drop services.

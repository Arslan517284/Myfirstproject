## 🚀 DEPLOY IN 3 STEPS

### STEP 1: Choose Your Deployment Platform

#### **Option A: Netlify (RECOMMENDED - Easiest)**
- **Time:** 2 minutes
- **Cost:** Free
- **Best For:** Quick deployment
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to https://netlify.com
  2. Click "Sign up" → Choose GitHub
  3. Authorize your GitHub account
  4. Click "New site from Git"
  5. Select repository: Myfirstproject
  6. Deploy!

#### **Option B: GitHub Pages (Free & Simple)**
- **Time:** 1 minute
- **Cost:** Free
- **Best For:** Permanent free hosting
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to: https://github.com/Arslan517284/Myfirstproject/settings
  2. Scroll to "Pages" section
  3. Select: Deploy from branch → main
  4. Wait 2 minutes
  5. Your site is live!

#### **Option C: Vercel (Fast & Modern)**
- **Time:** 2 minutes
- **Cost:** Free
- **Best For:** Performance & auto-updates
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to https://vercel.com
  2. Click "New Project"
  3. Import: Myfirstproject
  4. Click "Deploy"
  5. Done!
